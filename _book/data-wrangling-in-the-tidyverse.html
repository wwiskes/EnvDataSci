Chapter 5 Data Wrangling in the tidyverse

The goals of this section are to continue where we started in the earlier chapter on data manipulation with dplyr, taking it further to let us restructure our data with tidyr, including the use of pivot tables, as well as string methods in stringr, dates & times processing with lubridate, and dplyr methods for joining data, doing set operations and binding data frame sections together.
